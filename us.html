About Us

Welcome to MARYAM'S AROMA, your trusted destination for exquisite perfumes since 2002. Nestled in the heart of Valaichenai, a charming town in Sri Lanka's Eastern Province, we have been dedicated to bringing the finest fragrances to our customers for over two decades.

Our journey began with a simple vision - to offer the highest-quality, long-lasting perfumes that capture elegance, charm, and individuality. Over the years, we have carefully curated a collection of perfumes from renowned brands and unique niche fragrances, ensuring that there's something special for everyone.

With the rise of online shopping, we are proud to extend our passion for perfumes beyond Valaichenai, allowing fragrance lovers across the country to explore and shop with ease. Whether you seek a signature scent or a thoughtful gift, MARYAM'S AROMA is here to help you find the perfect fragrance for every occasion.

Thank you for being a part of our journey. We look forward to serving you with the finest perfumes and exceptional customer service!
